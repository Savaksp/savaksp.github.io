Commands

  • /bal [Optional user]

    Check your/someone else's balance.

  • /pay [Member user] [Number amount]

    Transfer currency to user.

  • /work [Text work]

    Earn some currency.

  • /rob

    Steal currency.

  • /shop

    View products in the shop.

  • /buy [Text item]

    Buy goods from the shop.
